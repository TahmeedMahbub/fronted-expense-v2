What is Remaining Adjustment?

Just calculate balance and turn ON the Remaining Adjustment. It will Automatically Calculate How much you expensed or income.

As an example, this app is showing balance of BDT 1000. You expensed in many purpose and now you cannot rememeber how much you expensed. But you know that, you have BDT 800 as your balance. Just put 800 and turn ON the switch of Remaining Adjustment. It will automatically calculate you expensed BDT 200. And took the entry you expensed, not your balance.
